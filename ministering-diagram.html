Ministering Flow

  1. Bishop presides and assigns oversight to EQ President and RS President
  2. Each president appoints Counselors responsible for ministering coordination
  3. Counselors manage Ministerial assignments (brothers/sisters with companions)
  4. Each pair is given Assigned Families to minister to
  5. Both EQ and RS track progress through interviews
  6. The final outcome and goal is to Inform Ward Council + LCR + Record all progress
